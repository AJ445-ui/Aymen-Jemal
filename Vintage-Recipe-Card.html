🍪Grandma`s Chocolate Chip Cookies🍪

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Shortening(or 1\2 cup Shortening + 1\2 cup Butter)
  • 1 cup Brown Suger
  • 1 cup white Suger
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Soda
  • 1\2 Tbsp Salt
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 3 Cups(12-oz bag) Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • (Optional: 1 cup chopped walnuts)

Prepartion

  • Preheat Oven to 375oF (190oC).
  • Cream Shortening(or butter mix) with brown and white Suger.
  • Add Eggs, Milk and Vanilla. Mix well.
  • Stir in Flour, Baking Soda and Salt.
  • Fold in Chocolate chips(walnuts if in use).
  • Drop Spoonfuls onto Baking Sheet.
  • Bake 8-10 minutes until golden.
  • Cool before serving

*With this recipe we can expect about 36-34 cookies
(around 4 dozen).*
